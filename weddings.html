What I need from you (Checklist)

  1. Final spellings — EXACT spelling for every name (capitalisation matters). Example: Sarah-Jane vs Sarah Jane.
  2. Name list file — Preferably a single .csv or .xlsx with columns: ItemID, Name, Role (optional), Placement (L/R/C).
  3. Font choice — Pick one of our preset fonts (send screenshot) or upload a licensed font file. If none chosen, we will use a clean modern sans-serif.
  4. Logo / Monogram — Vector (.ai/.eps/.svg) preferred. If only a raster image is available, we’ll need a high-res PNG/JPG.
  5. Cup type & colour — e.g. YETI Rambler 590ml, powder coat - Matte Black. If you want us to supply cups, say so for quote.
  6. Placement — Left, Right, Centre, or Wrap. Default = Centre-left (for right-hand hold).
  7. Quantity confirmation — Final count and any splits (e.g. 8 bridesmaids + 2 flower girls = 10 total).
  8. Delivery address & date — Where to send or if collection; when you need them by (production lead time starts after artwork approval).
  9. Approval method — Confirm whether you want a digital mock-up (PDF) for sign-off. Note: we require sign-off before mass production.
  10. Payment terms — 50% deposit required to lock order, balance before pickup/shipping.
